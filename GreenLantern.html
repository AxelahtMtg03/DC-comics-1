Profile

Green Lantern is an American comic strip created in 1940 and published by publisher DC Comics.

In the universe what we call the guardians we create the Green Lantern in order to protect the universe, this one is divided into 3600 sectors and there is a Green Lantern per sector. There are several human Green Lntern like Hal Jordan, Kyle Rayner, John Stewart, Guy Gardner, Simon Baz and Jessica Cruz.It is no one is chosen in the whole universe to defend their sector where they were born.The Green Lantern represents the will.

Abilities
The Green Lanterns have a lot of power like for example to speak all the known languages of the universe, to fly even in space, to breathe anywhere, little to materialize anything thanks to its ring and its ring can also generate a costume in energy.
Enemies
The most famous enemy of the Green Lanterns are the Yellow Lanterns otherwise called the Sinestro Corps but the Green Lanterns have other enemies like Dr Polaris, Evil Star, Goldface, Hector Hammond, Krona, Major Disaster, The Manhunters, Parallax, Shark , Sonar, Solomon Grundy, Star Sapphire and Tatooed Man.
Are there any other type of Lantern Corps?

Indeed there is another type of Lantern corps, in The War of the Corps of Sinestro comics, we see that there is in fact an Agent Corps for each of the seven traditional colors of the rainbow. Red for hate / rage, orange for greed / greed, yellow for fear, green for willpower, blue for hope, indigo for compassion, pink for love. The eighth body is black, for the absence of feeling and death. And the ninth, the white which represents life. Each one has different origins and different leaders and sectors, here are the nine examples:

The leader of the Rage Corps (red) is Atrocitus de Ryutt, who created the Red Lantern Corps on the planet of Ysmault (Sector 2814).

The leader of the Avarice (orange) corps is Larfleeze, coming from Okaara, in the Vega system (sector 2828).

The head of the fear body (yellow) is Sinestro, a native of Korugar, who bases the body on Qward (Sector 1 of the Antimatter Universe).

The head of the will body (green) are the Guardians, originally from Maltus, having based the body on Oa (sector 0).

The head of the Hope Corps (blue) is the couple Ganthet and Sayd, coming from Oa but having installed their corps on Odym (sector 2628).

The head of the body of compassion (indigo) is Indigo1 (or Iroque), coming from an unknown planet. The source of Indigo energy comes from the subsoil of the planet Nok (sector 2814).

The head of the body of love (purple) is Carol Ferris, coming from Earth but representing the Zamarones, native to Malthus as the Guardians. Since the separation of the Malthusians, they are based on Zamaron (sector 1416).

The head of the body of life (white) is the Entity, having always been on Earth (sector 2814).

The leader of the body of death (black) is Black Hand, a supervillain, he is also the only representative still active. (sector 666).

Each body represents an "emotional spectrum" each of which has its own "entity":

The entity of rabies is The Butcher,

The entity of greed is Ophidian,

The entity of fear is Parallax,

The entity of will is Ion,

The entity of hope is Adara,

The entity of compassion is Proselyte,

The entity of love is The Predator,

The entity of life is The Entity (the body of the White Lanterns does not exist, the Entity is both the representative of all the colors of the emotional spectrum resulting from the war between darkness and light at creation and creating thus the 7 colors of the emotional spectrum),

The entity of death is Nekron.
