Profile

Bruce Wayne, aka Batman, is a fictional superhero belonging to the DC Comics universe. Created by cartoonist Bob Kane and screenwriter Bill Finger, it first appeared in Detective Comics # 27 in 1939 - May 1939 as the cover date but the actual release date is March 30, 1939 - under the name of The Bat-Man.

Abilities

Batman is a grace his strength lies in his detective abilities, his intelligence, his physical abilities and access to cutting-edge technology. His training, his resources, his rigor thus largely compensate for the absence of a specific power. He is a master in many martial disciplines (especially with bare hands) which have varied over time. In the early days, Batman practiced judo and jiujitsu then karate and kung fu which he used from the 1950s to the 1970s. Since the 1980s, his fighting technique has been a mixture of different martial arts and his abilities. physical are worthy of an Olympic level. Devoid of any superpowers to fight crime, Batman relies solely on his physical condition and gadgets. His costume, designed to terrify criminals, is in Kevlar which protects him from bullets and bladed weapons while his nomex cloak protects him from fire. His belt includes several pockets in which he stores offensive and defensive weapons: batarangs which are boomerangs in the stylized shape of a bat, neutralizing gas capsules and a taser Batarangs can be thrown by hand or with a special gun. Some batarangs have a particular function (magnetic batarangs, equipped with a camera, light, explosives, etc.). A first aid kit is also slipped into his belt. Batman uses different costumes adapted to the particularities of the missions: diving in the high seas, moving in space, etc.

After using unmarked vehicles, Batman built the Batmobile first seen in 1941. The vehicle is armored and the front designed in the stylized form of a bat's head, can act as a ram. The model varies over the decades. In the 1950s, it was a roadster then a sedan, a sports car in the 1960s, a sports coupe for the 1970s, a dragster in the 1980s followed by a futuristic vehicle. This extraordinary vehicle is equipped with many gadgets and includes a mini criminology laboratory. The batmobile is Batman's best-known contraption, but it's not the only one. Depending on his needs, Batman uses a 20 batcyclen, batplane, batcopter, batboat, batsub and many more.
